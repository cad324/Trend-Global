About Us

Trend International USA Inc. was established on 1981.We are a fashion designing and also whole seller. Our main production is high quality jewelry. Most of all the merchandise is sold to American or European Markets. Our designs are very up to date with the current fashion for both markets. The factory is located in Guangzhou which is has the biggest jewelry production factories in China.

With more than 35 years of experience, from the beginning of design to final production of a piece of elegant jewelry, we design/produce high quality of jewelry products of sterling silver, 14K and 10K gold, & fashion jewelry with cubic zirconia, gemstones, diamonds, crystal, for women and men. Our clients/partners consist of wholesalers, retailers, designer and chain store representatives. We offer a wide variety of styles and products in jewelry.
